About Us

Manakamana Glass Fiber is a proudly Nepali company specializing in creative, durable, and innovative glass fiber products. We design and manufacture high-quality FRP structures that combine strength, beauty, and reliability. With two showrooms in Butwal (Mainavagar) and Kapilvastu (Imliya), we continue to serve homes, schools, and industries across Nepal.
